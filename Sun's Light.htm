Stare into the sky and sigh

Eyes sparkling in the Sun, making oceans rise and fall

Waves crash on your cheeks, mending into the sand at your feet

For the week you’ve carried a weight that pushed you down

In the mud you were born and you’ve fought your way out

Feeling the Sun kiss you all over.

 

Your tears whisper as they travel from your chin to the beach

Your toes wiggle and you feel the heat

Opposed to the cruelty you were exposed to for years

You cry for lost time, cry for relationships broken, discarded

Your soul refills in the light, the knife replaced with hands wrapped in promises.

 

We promise that your newfound freedom won’t be hindered by your past experiences

On the block selling dreams instead of demons, giving out wisdom instead of chains,

We appear in the depths of your mind to give you well wishes

Forgive but never forget, your old self is not in charge of your current well being

Avoid what imprisoned you and face tomorrow with your chin up.
